My Prompt Tester

Draft and test prompts and app/assistant instructions for Apple Intelligence. Copy the prompt, instruction, and response together in one tap—or save them as a set. Saved sets sync with iCloud so you can access them on Mac, iPhone, and iPad.

Download on the App Store

What you can do

  • Test Apple Intelligence prompts — experiment with phrasing and app/assistant instructions side‑by‑side.
  • One‑tap copy bundle — copy the prompt + instruction + response together to the clipboard.
  • Save reusable sets — keep prompt, instruction, and response as a named set for later.
  • iCloud sync — your saved sets stay in sync across Mac, iPhone, and iPad using your Apple ID.
  • Stay organized — duplicate sets to iterate quickly, add notes, and compare outcomes.
  • Privacy‑first — no accounts and no developer servers. Your content lives on your devices and in your iCloud.

Apple Intelligence availability varies by device and region. My Prompt Tester is an independent tool and is not affiliated with Apple.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 16, 2025

My Prompt Tester is built with privacy by design. Your prompts, instructions, and responses are processed on your device. We do not operate our own servers and we do not sell or share your data for advertising.

Data we store

  • On‑device data: Drafts and saved sets (prompt, instruction, response, and notes) are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud sync (optional): If enabled, saved sets are synced via Apple’s iCloud using your Apple ID. Data is stored in your iCloud account and governed by Apple’s terms. The developer cannot access your iCloud content.

Data we do not collect

  • No account registration, no usage analytics, and no third‑party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not transmit your prompts, instructions, or responses to developer‑controlled servers.

Clipboard & permissions

  • Clipboard: The app writes to your clipboard only when you explicitly use the copy action.
  • iCloud: Accessed solely to sync your saved sets between your own devices when you enable sync.

Crash reports

If you opt in to “Share with App Developers” in iOS/iPadOS/macOS settings, Apple may provide the developer with anonymized crash logs. These logs do not include your content.

Security

Your synced data is stored by Apple’s iCloud. We do not host servers holding your content.

Retention & control

Your saved sets remain until you delete them from the app or remove them from iCloud. Deleting the app does not automatically delete content stored in iCloud.

Changes

If future updates introduce features that involve external processing, this policy will be updated and you will be notified in the app or on this page.
